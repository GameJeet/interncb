How AuthLab Lite protects accounts

1

Password policy

Login passwords are checked for length, casing, digits and special characters before they’re accepted.

2

Rate limiting

After 5 failed attempts in a session, login is paused for 30 seconds with a live countdown.

3

Account lockout

Repeated failures on one account trigger a 2-minute lockout, independent of the session.

4

CAPTCHA challenge

After 3 failed attempts, a generated CAPTCHA must be solved to continue.
